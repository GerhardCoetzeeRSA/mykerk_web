VraeFAQ

Algemene Vrae

Common Questions

Moet ons personeel tegnies wees?Does our staff need to be technical?

Nee. Die admin-paneel is ontwerp vir enige gemeentelid wat 'n selfoon kan gebruik.

No. The admin panel is designed for any congregation member who can use a smartphone.

Kan ons enige tyd kanselleer?Can we cancel anytime?

Ja. Geen kontrak — betaal maandeliks of jaarliks en kanselleer wanneer jy wil.

Yes. No contract — pay monthly or annually and cancel whenever you'd like.

Hoe werk die tweetalige wisseling?How does the bilingual switch work?

Een druk op AF/EN wissel elke skerm onmiddellik — lidmate kies hul eie voorkeurtaal.

One tap on AF/EN switches every screen instantly — members choose their own preferred language.

Is ons data veilig?Is our data safe?

Ja. Elke gemeente kry sy eie beskermde profiel op Google Firebase-infrastruktuur.

Yes. Each congregation gets its own protected profile on Google Firebase infrastructure.

Voldoen MyKerk aan POPIA?Does MyKerk comply with POPIA?

MyKerk is gebou met POPIA in gedagte. Data word in 'n geïsoleerde, toegang-beheerde profiel gestoor. Jou gemeente bly egter die verantwoordelike party en moet steeds toestemming van lidmate verkry.

MyKerk is built with POPIA in mind. Data is stored in an isolated, access-controlled profile. Your congregation remains the responsible party and should still obtain member consent.

Watter betaalopsies is beskikbaar?What payment options are available?

Ons aanvaar betaling via Zapper QR-kode. Ander betaalopsies kom binnekort.

We accept payment via Zapper QR code. More payment options coming soon.
